Keton Kakkar

500 College Avenue · Swarthmore, PA 19081 · ketonkakkar [at] gmail [dot] com

I am a journalist and computer scientist interested in the ethical implications and societal impacts of big tech. I am the managing editor of The Phoenix, Swarthmore College's independent student newspaper, and have been working in college journalism since September 2015. I have research experience in supervised machine learning and have interned at Harvard's Berkman Klein Center for Internet and Society. Additionally, I have worked in the New York real estate industry as a title closer and am a licensed Mortgage Loan Originator.

For fun, I write fiction, climb rocks, and publish Small Craft Warnings, Swarthmore's oldest literary magazine.
